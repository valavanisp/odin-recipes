Freddo Espresso

The Greek Freddo Espresso

The Freddo Espresso, although Italian in name, is a standard beverage done in Greece. It is a little more involved to make compared to a standard frappe, but very worth it.

Ingredients

  • Ground espresso
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Water
  • Milk, preferably evaporated from the can

Steps

  1. Pull a shot of espresso and pour it in your serving glass
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of sugar for mildly sweet, 2 teaspoons for sweet
  3. With a frother, from the mix until it is smooth and a nice head of foam has developed
  4. Add ice
  5. Insert straw, and enjoy!

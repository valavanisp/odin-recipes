Greek Frappe

The classic Greek Frappe

The classic Greek Frappe is a standard in any household as an easy, quick offering to any guests. It is easy to make, and it requires very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee, preferably Nescafe
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Water
  • Milk, preferably evaporated from the can

Steps

  1. Add 1 to 1.5 teaspoons of instant coffee in the glass
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of sugar for mildly sweet, 2 teaspoons for sweet
  3. Add "2 fingers" of water to cover the mix and have some space above it with water
  4. With a frother, from the mix until it is smooth and a nice head of foam has developed
  5. Add ice
  6. Add milk to taste
  7. Top off with water
  8. Insert straw, and enjoy!
